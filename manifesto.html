Hernan Diaz Alonso / Xefirotarch: Manifesto #19

How boring has perfection become?

Here that sensation is more like painting than engineering.

The horrific-becoming-beautiful and the beautiful-becoming-horrific (grotesque)

We are becoming monsters – a species aroused by non-perfect images coming from perfect methodologies.

So why not be optimistic? In the end such is life, such is love…
